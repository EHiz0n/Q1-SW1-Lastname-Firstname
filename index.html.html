The Art of Muay Thai

Muay Thai is a martial art originating in Thailand and it is originally derived from Muay Boran, the forerunner of Muay Thai. Muay Thai is one of the many common martial arts within the fighting background and is known to be lethal to the opponent and oneself compared to other martial art styles like Boxing and Taekwando.

Why I practice Muay Thai

  • It's a good workout, allowing me to exercise more and be active
  • I can learn to defend myself and fight with proper form
  • My muscles, bones, and stamina can get stronger

A well known Muay Thai fighter

Rodtang, A.K.A the Iron Man, is my favorite Muay Thai fighter and is nicknamed the Iron Man due to his resistance against punches and kicks, seemingly unable to be knocked out or damaged as he eats all of them up like they were ticklish. This was because of his extensive neck and body training which helped him gain resistance.
